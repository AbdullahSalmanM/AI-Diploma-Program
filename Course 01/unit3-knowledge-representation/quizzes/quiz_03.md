## Part 2: Short Answer (30 points)

### Question 2 (15 points)
Explain the difference between forward and backward chaining in rule-based systems.

**Answer Key:**
- Forward chaining: Starts with facts, applies rules to derive new facts (data-driven)
- Backward chaining: Starts with goal, works backwards to find supporting facts (goal-driven)
